Ашихмина Татьяна Валентиновна, Головко Артем Александрович , Ткаченко Алла Мансуровна , Рябых Людмила Викторовна , Титов Олег Александрович

Аннотация. Пожары на полигонах твердых коммунальных отходов происходят ежегодно и составляют примерно 14% от общего количества пожаров в РФ. Химические компоненты дымовых газов, так же, как и составляющие биогаза до момента возгорания, являются значимыми факторами воздействия на различные компоненты окружающей среды, в первую очередь, атмосферу. Целью исследования является прогнозирование воздействия эмиссионных процессов, сопровождающих горение полигонов твердых коммунальных отходов (ТКО) Воронежской области, на геоэкологические условия. В работе предложен методический подход к прогнозированию геоэкологического воздействия процессов горения отходов на полигонах ТКО, включающий поэтапный анализ факторов и процесса формирования такого воздействия, а также расчет количественных параметров выбросов продуктов горения на объектах исследования, моделирование пространственного распределения токсичных выбросов, построение вероятностных моделей рассеивания токсичных веществ при горении отходов на полигоне. Расчеты и моделирование производилось на основе базы метеоданных Всемирной Метеорологической Организации (WMO), усредненных технико-эксплуатационных параметров полигонов. Полученные результаты позволяют оценить масштабы выбросов и их пространственное распространение, проанализировать геоэкологическое воздействие преобладающих компонентов выбросов. Практическое применение вероятностных моделей зон токсического задымления заключается в детализации системы производственного экологического контроля (мониторинга) на этапе проектирования полигона ТКО, а также в осуществлении оперативного контроля концентраций загрязняющих веществ на территории воздействия пожара. Предложенный подход к прогнозированию геоэкологического воздействия горения ТКО рекомендуется применять для построения схемы геоэкологического мониторинга территории расположения полигонов ТКО.

Ключевые слова: горение отходов, полигон ТКО, прогнозирование геоэкологического воздействия, пространственное моделирование зоны токсического задымления, геоэкологический мониторинг.

FORECASTING THE GEOECOLOGICAL IMPACT OF FIRES AT SOLID WASTE LANDFILLS IN THE VORONEZH REGION

Ashikhmina T.V., Golovko A.A., Tkachenko A.M., Ryabykh L.V., Titov O. A.

Voronezh State Technical University, Voronezh, Russia

Abstract. Fires at solid waste landfills occur annually, accounting for approximately 14% of all fires in Russia. The chemical components of smoke emissions, as well as the biogas constituents prior to ignition, are significant factors that impact various components of the environment, particularly the atmosphere. This study aims to forecast the impact of emission processes accompanying the combustion of solid waste landfills in the Voronezh region on geoecological conditions. The work proposes a methodological approach to predict the geoecological impact of waste combustion processes at solid waste landfills, which includes a step-by-step analysis of factors and processes contributing to this impact. It also involves the calculation of quantitative parameters of combustion product emissions at the study sites, modeling the spatial distribution of toxic emissions, and constructing probabilistic models of the dispersion of toxic substances during landfill fires. The calculations and modeling were based on the World Meteorological Organization's (WMO) meteorological database and averaged technical and operational parameters of the landfills. The obtained results allow for assessing the scale of emissions and their spatial distribution, as well as analyzing the geoecological impact of the predominant emission components. The practical application of probabilistic models for toxic smoke zones lies in refining the industrial environmental control system (monitoring) during the design phase of solid waste landfills, as well as in conducting real-time monitoring of pollutant concentrations in the affected areas during fires. The proposed approach for forecasting the geoecological impact of solid waste combustion is recommended for developing a scheme of geoecological monitoring in the areas where solid waste landfills are located.

Key words: waste combustion, solid waste landfill, forecasting geoecological impact, spatial modeling of toxic smoke zones, geoecological monitoring.

в астраханской области

Сокольский Аркадий Федорович 1 , Боронина Людмила Владимировна 2 , Медведев Алексей Александрович 3 , Сокольская Евгения Аркадьевна 4

1 Астраханский государственный архитектурно-строительный университет,

2 Федеральное государственное бюджетное образовательное учреждение высшего образования «Национальный исследовательский московский государственный строительный университет (НИУ МГСУ), 3 Муниципальное унитарное предприятие г. Астрахани «Астрводоканал», 4 Астраханский государственный университет,

Аннотация. В последние десятилетия заметно изменился химический состав поверхностных вод. Причинами изменения стали не только интенсивное антропогенное воздействие в результате сброса неочищенных сточных вод в водоисточники и залповых аварийных выбросов, но и активный рост сине-зеленых водорослей. Следствием сложившейся ситуации стало стремительное ухудшение качества воды источника и отрицательное изменение качества питьевой воды. Запроектированные в 30-70-хх годах прошлого столетия водоочистные сооружения города Астрахани не были рассчитаны на сложные экологические условия эксплуатации, поэтому в настоящее время перестали справляться с возложенными на них функциями. В статье приводятся результаты исследований по доочистке питьевой воды от антропогенных и природных примесей с применением порошкообразного и гранулированного активированного угля.

Ключевые слова : водоисточник, доочистка питьевой воды, сине-зеленые водоросли, экологическая безопасность питьевой воды.

Abstract: The chemical composition of surface waters has changed markedly in recent decades. The reasons for the change were not only the intense anthropogenic impact as a result of the discharge of untreated wastewater into water sources and salvo emergency emissions, but also the active growth of blue-green algae. The consequence of this situation was a rapid deterioration of the water quality of the source and a negative change in the quality of drinking water. The water treatment facilities of Astrakhan, designed in the 30-70s of the last century, were not designed for difficult environmental operating conditions, therefore, they have now ceased to cope with the functions assigned to them. In the article, the authors present the results of research on the purification of drinking water from anthropogenic and natural impurities using powdered and granular activated carbon.

DIGITAL MODELING OF THE RELIEF TAMBOV REGION ON THE EXAMPLE MORSHANSKY MUNICIPAL DISTRICT

Bukovskiy Mikhail Evgenievich, Kuzmin Kirill Alekseevich

Tambov State University named after G.R. Derzhavin, Tambov, Russia

Abstract: The tasks of organizing ecologically balanced agriculture, which implies minimizing the risks of degradation of the soil cover and other components of the natural environment, require studying the geomorphological basis of agrolandscapes and obtaining quantitative variables of the relief. The article considers the issues of digital modeling and morphometric analysis of the relief of the Tambov region using the example of the Morshansky municipal district. To analyze the spatial distribution of morphometric variables on a regional scale, a digital elevation model with a spatial resolution of 30 m was created based on topographic maps. At the level of rural territorial entities of the Morshansky municipal district, the variables of the steepness and aspects of slopes, vertical and horizontal dissection of the relief were considered. The results of the morphometric analysis of the relief can be used as a basis for design decisions on land management and the introduction of resource-saving farming systems within a separate region.

Keywords: Digital elevation model, morphometric analysis, land use, soil erosion, Tambov region.

Annotation. The Volga-Caspian Sea Shipping Channel (VKMSK) has been a connecting link connecting Europe's largest river, the Volga, with the Caspian Sea for the second hundred years. This is a unique hydraulic engineering facility, which, under the conditions of modern sanctions by Western countries, is considered one of the strategically important ones for both the economy of the Caspian region and the economy of Russia as a whole. The issues of navigation in this area are of particular importance in connection with the development of the North-South transport corridor, in which the lower reaches of the Volga, the VKMSK and the shallow northern part of the Caspian Sea are the most difficult for navigation. Increasing the capacity of the ports of Astrakhan and Olya, increasing ship loading and cargo turnover is really feasible only if stable year-round navigation at the junction of the Volga River and the Caspian Sea. Every year in winter, the entire Volga, including the waters of the ports of Astrakhan and Olya, as well as the Volga-Caspian Sea shipping Channel and the northern part of the Caspian Sea are covered with ice, and the ice cover, representing water in its solid state, is much more aggressive, and represents a serious obstacle to navigation.

Keywords: ice formation, winter navigation, Lower Volga, Volga-Caspian Sea shipping channel, Northern Caspian, hydrometeorological situation, safety of navigation.

INVERTEBRATES LISTED IN THE RED BOOK OF THE ASTRAKHAN REGION: PROBLEMS OF STUDY AND PROTECTION

Chuikov Yuri, Chuikova Lyudmila,

Astrakhan State University named after V.N. Tatishchev

The new (third) edition of the Red Book of the Astrakhan Region includes 82 species of invertebrates. Of these, 4 species are assigned to the zero category of rarity status; 14 species belong to the 2nd category; 12 species were added to the 3rd; to the 4th – 28 widows, to the 5th – 5 species. It is quite difficult to organize the protection of individual species. The solution to the problem can be the protection of rare species in specially protected natural areas of different levels. There are two state nature reserves on the territory of the Astrakhan region - Astrakhan (biosphere, created in 1919) and Bogdinsko-Baskunchak (created in 1997). A significant number of rare species of invertebrates are protected on their territory. A particularly important role in their protection is played by the Bogdinsko-Baskunchak State Nature Reserve. Its territory is home to 28 species of invertebrates listed in the regional Red Book (34% of their total number). 10 species are found only here. The analysis of the above information showed that it is necessary to create new protected areas, specially designed for the protection of rare species of invertebrates. First of all, it is necessary to pay attention to the territory adjacent to the village of Dosang. 14 rare species of insects (17% of all rare insects in the region) have been found here. Of these, 6 species are found only here.
