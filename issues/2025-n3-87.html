ПРОГНОЗИРОВАНИЕ ГЕОЭКОЛОГИЧЕСКОГО ВОЗДЕЙСТВИЯ ПОЖАРОВ НА ПОЛИГОНАХ ТКО ВОРОНЕЖСКОЙ ОБЛАСТИ

Ашихмина Татьяна Валентиновна, Головко Артем Александрович , Ткаченко Алла Мансуровна , Рябых Людмила Викторовна , Титов Олег Александрович

Федеральное государственное бюджетное образовательное учреждение высшего образования "Воронежский государственный технический университет", г. Воронеж, Россия

Аннотация. Пожары на полигонах твердых коммунальных отходов происходят ежегодно и составляют примерно 14% от общего количества пожаров в РФ. Химические компоненты дымовых газов, так же, как и составляющие биогаза до момента возгорания, являются значимыми факторами воздействия на различные компоненты окружающей среды, в первую очередь, атмосферу. Целью исследования является прогнозирование воздействия эмиссионных процессов, сопровождающих горение полигонов твердых коммунальных отходов (ТКО) Воронежской области, на геоэкологические условия. В работе предложен методический подход к прогнозированию геоэкологического воздействия процессов горения отходов на полигонах ТКО, включающий поэтапный анализ факторов и процесса формирования такого воздействия, а также расчет количественных параметров выбросов продуктов горения на объектах исследования, моделирование пространственного распределения токсичных выбросов, построение вероятностных моделей рассеивания токсичных веществ при горении отходов на полигоне. Расчеты и моделирование производилось на основе базы метеоданных Всемирной Метеорологической Организации (WMO), усредненных технико-эксплуатационных параметров полигонов. Полученные результаты позволяют оценить масштабы выбросов и их пространственное распространение, проанализировать геоэкологическое воздействие преобладающих компонентов выбросов. Практическое применение вероятностных моделей зон токсического задымления заключается в детализации системы производственного экологического контроля (мониторинга) на этапе проектирования полигона ТКО, а также в осуществлении оперативного контроля концентраций загрязняющих веществ на территории воздействия пожара. Предложенный подход к прогнозированию геоэкологического воздействия горения ТКО рекомендуется применять для построения схемы геоэкологического мониторинга территории расположения полигонов ТКО.

Ключевые слова: горение отходов, полигон ТКО, прогнозирование геоэкологического воздействия, пространственное моделирование зоны токсического задымления, геоэкологический мониторинг.

Для цитирования: Ашихмина Т.В., Головко А.А. Ткаченко А.М., Рябых Л.В., Титов О.А. Прогнозирование геоэкологического воздействия пожаров на полигонах ТКО Воронежской области // Астраханский вестник экологического образования. 2025, №3 (87). С . 4-15 DOI 10.36698/2304-5957-2025-3-4-15

FORECASTING THE GEOECOLOGICAL IMPACT OF FIRES AT SOLID WASTE LANDFILLS IN THE VORONEZH REGION

Ashikhmina T.V., Golovko A.A., Tkachenko A.M., Ryabykh L.V., Titov O. A.

Voronezh State Technical University, Voronezh, Russia

Abstract. Fires at solid waste landfills occur annually, accounting for approximately 14% of all fires in Russia. The chemical components of smoke emissions, as well as the biogas constituents prior to ignition, are significant factors that impact various components of the environment, particularly the atmosphere. This study aims to forecast the impact of emission processes accompanying the combustion of solid waste landfills in the Voronezh region on geoecological conditions. The work proposes a methodological approach to predict the geoecological impact of waste combustion processes at solid waste landfills, which includes a step-by-step analysis of factors and processes contributing to this impact. It also involves the calculation of quantitative parameters of combustion product emissions at the study sites, modeling the spatial distribution of toxic emissions, and constructing probabilistic models of the dispersion of toxic substances during landfill fires. The calculations and modeling were based on the World Meteorological Organization's (WMO) meteorological database and averaged technical and operational parameters of the landfills. The obtained results allow for assessing the scale of emissions and their spatial distribution, as well as analyzing the geoecological impact of the predominant emission components. The practical application of probabilistic models for toxic smoke zones lies in refining the industrial environmental control system (monitoring) during the design phase of solid waste landfills, as well as in conducting real-time monitoring of pollutant concentrations in the affected areas during fires. The proposed approach for forecasting the geoecological impact of solid waste combustion is recommended for developing a scheme of geoecological monitoring in the areas where solid waste landfills are located.

Key words: waste combustion, solid waste landfill, forecasting geoecological impact, spatial modeling of toxic smoke zones, geoecological monitoring.

For citation: Ashikhmina T.V., Golovko A.A., Tkachenko A.M., Ryabykh L.V., Titov O.A. Forecasting the geoecological impact of fires at solid waste landfills in the Voronezh region. // Astrakhan Bulletin of Ecological Education. 2025, №3 (87): 4-15 DOI 10.36698/2304-5957-2025-3-4-15

Dega Natalia S., Adzhiev Ruslan I., Bayramkulova Asiyat R.

Umar Aliev Karachay-Cherkessia State University

Abstract: The article is devoted to the analysis of theoretical, methodological and legal aspects of the integration of geoinformation technologies and artificial intelligence methods into precision farming systems in Russia. The analysis of the regulatory framework has been carried out and the main barriers to the introduction of digital solutions in the agro-industrial complex have been identified: lack of agricultural data standards, shortage of qualified specialists, insufficient digital infrastructure. The international experience of digitalization of agriculture is evaluated, recommendations are proposed for creating a unified digital platform for agricultural data, improving legislation and supporting domestic developers. The research results can be used to form a state strategy for the digital transformation of agriculture and the introduction of intelligent technologies in agriculture.

Vaichulis Angelika Vissarionovna

Astrakhan Tatishchev State University, Astrakhan, Russia

Abstract: This article discusses the issue of mapping the reservoirs of the Western Ilmen River basins using the example of the Narimanovsky district. The reservoirs of the studied area have undergone significant changes over the past twenty years, due to the increasing impact of anthropogenesis. As a result, many Ilmen underwent salinization, overgrowth, and area changes. This article provides a brief description of the study area and the stages of creating a map using the QGIS 3.18.4 GIS program. The proposed electronic maps provide information about the territorial location, changes in areas and overgrowth of ilmen trees in the territory of Narimanovsky district.

STUDY OF LANDSCAPE DIFFERENTIATION PROPERTIES IN THE NORTH-EASTERN PART OF THE LESSER CAUCASUS

Jafarova L a tafat Gusan

Ganja State University, Ganja, Azerbaijan

Abstract. Increased population density on the north-eastern slope of the Lesser Caucasus, especially in mountain-forest and mountain-meadow landscapes, creates serious environmental risks in terms of transformation of natural landscapes. Dense population density in steep-slope mountain ranges further increases this risk. This problem poses a significant threat to mid-mountain and high-mountain areas, especially to areas with steep slopes and peaks. The article discusses the features of landscape differentiation processes in this region and their scientific and theoretical significance.

Among the methodologies and methods used in the study of geographical landscapes, the cartographic method, field research, geographical forecasting method and mathematical-statistical method were widely used. In the process of work, scientific papers on the topic, maps, Internet resources and statistical indicators were actively used.

The main scientific innovation put forward in the study is that as a result of anthropogenically conditioned differentiation processes in mountain-forest, forest-shrub and forest-meadow landscapes of the north-eastern part of the Lesser Caucasus, specific risk factors were identified with the corresponding factual data. Based on the conducted research, quantitative indicators of landscapes subjected to differentiation were determined.

Keywords: north-eastern part of the Lesser Caucasus, alpine, subalpine landscapes, differentiation.

Аннотация: в статье представлены результаты изучения влияния разных типов батареек на рост и развитие растений, на примере Vigna Savi . Для эксперимента были взяты три вида батареек – солевая, литиевая и алкалиновая. С каждым видом батареек был проведен опыт в двух вариантах – целой батарейкой и надломленной. В результате эксперимента было выявлено, что наиболее пагубное воздействие показали литиевые и алакалиновые батарейки. Вещества, содержащиеся в алкалиновых батарейках, не только замедляют развитие растений, но и вызывают деформацию его отдельных частей. Растение, развивающееся в почве, загрязненной алкалиновыми батарейками, выросло слабым, не способным удерживать свой вес, с изогнутыми и бугристыми листьями.

STUDYING THE EFFECTS OF BATTERIES ON PLANT GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT

Manjieva Tatiana Batrovna, Badmaeva Aigul Erdnievna, Chenkaleeva Dayana Baatrovna, Shalburova Aruna Bembevna, Davaeva Tsagan Dorjievna, Sangadzhieva Lyudmila Khalgaevna

Kalmyk State University Named after B.B. Gorodovikov

Abstract: The article presents the results of studying the effects of different types of batteries on plant growth and development, using the example of Vigna Savi. For the experiment, three types of batteries were used – salt, lithium and alkaline. An experiment was conducted with each type of battery in two versions – a whole battery and a broken one. As a result of the experiment, it was revealed that lithium batteries showed the most harmful effects. The lithium and other elements contained in them prevented Vigna Savi and its root system from fully developing. The substances contained in alkaline batteries not only slow down the development of plants, but also cause deformation of its individual parts. The plant, which develops in soil contaminated with alkaline batteries, has grown weak, unable to hold its weight, with curved and bumpy leaves.

Keywords: battery, soil salinization, salt batteries, lithium batteries, alkaline batteries.

For citation: Mandzhieva T.B., Badmaeva A.E., Chenkaleeva D.B., Shalburova A.B., Davaeva Ts.D., Sangadzhieva L.H. Studying the effects of batteries on plant growth and development // Astrakhan Bulletin of Ecological Education. 2025, №3 (87): 92-98 DOI 10.36698/2304-5957-2025-3-92-98
